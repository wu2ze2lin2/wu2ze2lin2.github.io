Biography

Now I'm a lecturer at Taiyuan University of Technology. I got my doctor degree at Taiyuan University of Technology in 2024, under the supervision of Prof. Xueying Zhang. I obtained my bachelor degree in Communication Engineering at Taiyuan University of Technology in 2020.

My research lies at the areas of medical image segmentation and deep learning. In particular, my current focus is mainly on stroke lesion segmentation, vision transformer, cross-modal learning.
